      Boutique Cyber Tonecraft

      We Build Instruments for the Neon Age

      KnaveTone started as a late‑night obsession in a tiny workshop — chasing the perfect blend of analog soul and digital precision. Today, we hand‑select every piece of wood, every pickup, and every line of code that powers our cyber‑enhanced guitars, basses, and rigs.

      Our mission: craft instruments that feel like classics but respond like next‑gen machines.

      From underground gigs to studio sessions, KnaveTone rigs are tuned for players who want clarity, attitude, and reliability in any signal chain.

      From Basement Lab to Boutique Brand

      What started as a single custom build for a friend quickly spiraled into a full‑time craft. We noticed modern players wanted more than off‑the‑shelf instruments — they wanted pieces that felt personal, looked futuristic, and still sounded like the records they grew up on.

      Every KnaveTone instrument is set up, tested, and play‑checked by real musicians before it ever hits the store. No dusty warehouse stock. No “good enough.” Only gear we’d be proud to take on stage ourselves.

      Why Players Choose KnaveTone ⚡
